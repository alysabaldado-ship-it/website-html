About Our School

Bais City National Science High School is a secondary public science high school located along Maria Longa Drive, Bais City, Negros Oriental. It is situated near the Bais City Fire Station and the LTO Bais District Office. The school is committed to providing quality education, fostering creativity, and shaping students into future leaders who are equipped with knowledge, skills, and values for lifelong success.

Dedicated to nurturing excellence, the school offers learners opportunities to go beyond the regular curriculum. One of its highlights is the annual Research Congress, participated in by students from Grades 9, 10, and 12. This activity allows students to showcase their research outputs, sharpening their academic foundation while cultivating critical thinking, creativity, and innovation.
